TCPA Violation Evaluator

Telephone Consumer Protection Act (47 U.S.C. § 227(b)(1)(A)(iii))

1
Was there a call or text?
2
Was the call/text sent to a cell phone number?
3
Did the caller use an automatic telephone dialing system (ATDS) OR an artificial/prerecorded voice?
4
Was the call made for emergency purposes?
5
Did the called party give prior express consent for that call/text?
6
Is the plaintiff the subscriber or regular user of the number called?
